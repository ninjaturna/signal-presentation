# Netflix × Launch — AI Enablement Brief

**Client:** Netflix
**Industry:** Media & Entertainment / Streaming
**Engagement type:** Opportunity pitch
**Stakeholders:** Chief Product Officer, VP of Personalization Engineering

---

## The challenge

Netflix has 260M+ subscribers globally but personalization is still primarily
driven by viewing history. It doesn't account for mood, time of day, social
context, or the gap between what people search for and what they actually finish.

Recommendation accuracy is high. Satisfaction with recommendations is flat.
The data exists. The understanding doesn't.

---

## Key priorities

- Reduce subscriber churn in the 6–18 month cohort
- Improve "content discovery to play" conversion rate
- Reduce decision fatigue on the home screen
- Develop a content intelligence layer that informs commissioning decisions

---

## The opportunity

An AI-powered Viewer Intelligence Platform that moves Netflix from
behavioral prediction to contextual understanding — connecting mood signals,
social viewing patterns, and content metadata to create recommendations that
feel genuinely personal rather than statistically probable.

---

## Suggested narrative arc

Open with the gap between Netflix's data advantage and subscriber satisfaction
scores. Show that the constraint is model architecture, not data volume.
Introduce the Viewer Intelligence Platform as the layer that closes the gap.
Close with a phased roadmap and a clear Phase 1 scope.
