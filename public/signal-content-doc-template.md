## Writing Guide (SIGNAL Brand Voice)

Before filling in slides, read these rules:
- **One idea per sentence.** Split long sentences in two.
- **Lead with the number or the outcome**, not the category.
  ✅ "87% adoption in 90 days." ❌ "We achieved high adoption quickly."
- **Present tense.** "Your team builds faster" not "will build faster."
- **Cut the last sentence.** Every slide is better without its summary line.
- **Max 15 words** for headings. **Max 40 words** for body copy on slides.
