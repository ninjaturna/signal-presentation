## Writing Rules (Read Before Filling In)

These rules apply to every field in this document.

- **One idea per sentence.** If you need a comma to finish a thought, use two sentences.
- **Lead with the number or outcome.** "87% adoption in 90 days." Not "We achieved high adoption."
- **Present tense always.** "Your team builds faster." Not "will build faster."
- **Cut the last sentence.** Every slide is better without its summary line.
- **Max 15 words** for HEADING. **Max 40 words** for BODY copy per slide.
- **Specific over vague.** Name the company, the number, the timeframe.

**Forbidden words:** leverage, synergy, ecosystem, seamless, robust, empower,
utilize, innovative, solution, comprehensive, streamline, cutting-edge.
